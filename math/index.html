정적분 문제

$$\begin{aligned} I = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx \end{aligned}$$


$$\begin{aligned} I^2 &= \left(\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx\right)^2 \\ &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx \cdot \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-y^2} dy \\ &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-(x^2+y^2)} dx\,dy \\ \end{aligned}$$


$$\begin{aligned} x &= r\cos \theta \\ y &= r\sin \theta \\ dx\,dy &= r\,dr\,d\theta \end{aligned}$$


$$\begin{aligned} I^2 &= \int_0^{2\pi}\int_0^\infty e^{-r^2} r\,dr\,d\theta \\ &= 2\pi \int_0^\infty e^{-r^2} r\,dr \\ &= 2\pi \left[-\frac{e^{-r^2}}{2}\right]_{r=0}^{r=\infty} \\ &= {\pi} \end{aligned}$$


$$\begin{aligned} I = \sqrt \pi \end{aligned}$$
